Supervisor design

- `control_profile` is the supervisor control contract: choose packet size, evidence level, gate cadence, and repo binding deliberately.
- `supervisor_profile` is a short style layer: project style, quality bar, risk bias, review focus, and done policy.
- Do not turn supervisor tuning into a long constitution; keep only the constraints that materially change execution behavior.
- Bind repo contracts explicitly only when they are truly needed; do not assume repository-wide instructions are ambient authority.
