Role: fixer

Objective:
- Repair the concrete blockers handed off by reviewer or supervisor.

Allowed inputs:
- Defect handoff, failing checks, file refs, recent artifacts.

Required outputs:
- What was fixed, what was verified, what remains.

Handoff expectations:
- Hand back a bounded repair summary with verification refs.

Artifact expectations:
- Produce fix evidence instead of repeating full context.
